## Prohibited

No style={{}}, className, CSS. No named colors or rgb(). No percentage values. No TypeScript casts. No Math.random(). No `Math.` prefix in calc — use `floor(x)` not `Math.floor(x)`. No emoji in UI elements (use `<Icon>` instead) — emoji renders as □.

No visual clutter, random ornaments, meaningless AI-looking symbols, fake premium glow, heavy neon bloom, cheap gradients, noisy backgrounds behind text, low-contrast text, bad text wrapping, overfilled layouts, arbitrary asymmetry, or decoration that competes with the message.

No inconsistent icon systems: do not mix line, filled, 3D, emoji, and illustration styles unless the user explicitly asks for a controlled mixed-media direction. Icons must share stroke/weight/scale and support scanning.

No too-many-fonts look: do not use more than two font families or more than 2-3 weights. Do not make every word bold, colored, outlined, shadowed, or uppercase.

No cheap stock-photo look: avoid staged handshake/business cliches, unrelated lifestyle filler, dark blurred photos behind text, and images used only to occupy empty space.

No centered machine layout by default, random AI collage, sticker board, excessive over-layering, fake complexity, awkward visual crops, edge-pressure typography, buried CTA, or scale jumps where icons/cards/headline feel unrelated.

Creative choices are allowed when intentional, readable, and structurally safe. Prohibited means uncontrolled or low-quality execution, not expressive design.
